## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Power BI dashboard** for analyzing customer behavior, spending patterns, and credit card utilization for Mitron Bank.

The goal is to derive **data-driven insights** to help the bank improve customer targeting, increase revenue, and optimize credit card offerings.

---
## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer demographics and behavior
* Identify high-value customer segments
* Understand spending patterns and trends
* Improve credit card utilization strategies
* Provide actionable business recommendations

---
## 📊 Key Insights

### 🔹 Customer Overview

* Total Customers: **4,000**
* Average Income: **₹51,657**
* Avg Monthly Spend: **₹22,120**
* Utilization Rate: **42.82%**

👉 Credit Cards dominate with the highest transaction value compared to UPI & Debit Cards 

---
### 🔹 Demographics Analysis

* Age Group **25–34** is the largest segment
* Gender Distribution:

  * Male: **64.9%**
  * Female: **35.1%**
* Top Cities: **Mumbai, Chennai, Bengaluru**

---
### 🔹 Income Utilization

* Highest utilization seen in **Salaried IT Employees**
* Married customers spend more than single customers

---
### 🔹 Spending Patterns
Top Categories:
* Bills: **₹105M**
* Groceries: **₹86M**
* Electronics: **₹80M**

📈 Peak spending during **August & September (Festive Season)**
