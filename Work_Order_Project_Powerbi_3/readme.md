## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **work order operations data** to understand service demand, technician utilization, revenue trends, and operational efficiency.

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** is created to help businesses make **data-driven decisions** and improve service performance.

---
## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze work order volume and service demand
* Identify high-performing districts
* Optimize technician allocation
* Understand cost and revenue patterns
* Improve operational efficiency

---
## 📊 Key Insights

### 🔹 Overall Performance

* Total Work Orders: **1,000K**
* Average Wait Time: **28.02**
* Rush Jobs: **10.3%**
* Total Revenue: **$236.6K**

👉 Indicates stable operations with moderate rush demand 

---
### 🔹 Service Analysis

* Highest demand: **Assessment Services**
* Medium demand: Replace & Deliver
* Lower demand: Repair & Install

---
### 🔹 District Performance

* Top districts: **Central & North**
* Low activity: Southwest & Northeast

👉 Service demand is geographically concentrated

---
### 🔹 Technician Analysis

* Most jobs require **1–2 technicians**
* Installation & Repair require more workforce

---
### 🔹 Warranty & Payment

* Majority jobs: **No warranty**
* Common payments: **Account & COD**

---
### 🔹 Cost & Revenue

* High revenue services: **Install & Repair**
* Low cost services: **Deliver**

---
## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase technicians in high-demand districts
* Promote maintenance packages to reduce rush jobs
* Improve performance in low-activity areas
* Optimize pricing strategies
* Encourage digital payments

---
